task_type Mission type enum_int {"0":"Immediate mission","1":"Timed mission","2":" Conditional mission"} Immediate and timed tasks are both specified with the execute_time for execution. If they are all satisfied, a flighttask_ready event notification will be triggered. Additionally, when the device receives the flighttask_execute command, it will also check if all the task's ready_conditions have been met.
